Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our club.

How can I join Rotaract?

You can join by clicking the "REGISTER" button at the top and filling out the form. Our membership team will contact you for an interview.

Is there an age limit?

Yes, Rotaract is designed for young leaders aged 18 to 30 who want to make a difference in their community.

What are the main activities?

We focus on 4 avenues: Community Service, International Service, Vocational Service (career growth), and Club Service (bonding).

Is it a job?

Rotaract is a 100% voluntary non-profit organization. We work for the passion of helping others and developing our leadership skills.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes! Active members participate in our projects and receive certificates that boost their CV and prove their community impact.

Can I suggest ideas?

Absolutely! We encourage all members to pitch new ideas that serve the community, and we help bring them to life.

Got more questions? We're just a message away!
