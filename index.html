Rotaract Renaissance Lumina is a youth-led service club in Alexandria , Egypt. dedicated to community service, leadership, and personal development.

Who We Are

Leading Change,
Serving Humanity

Rotaract Renaissance Lumina is a community of young leaders in Alexandria dedicated to professional development and impactful community service. We believe in the power of "Service Above Self."

Community Service

Executing sustainable projects that impact our local community through dedicated volunteer work.

Professional Growth

Building leadership skills and providing networking opportunities for our members' future careers.

Our Core Vision

To empower young adults to create positive, lasting change in themselves and their communities through fellowship and service.
